sanxia
2024
In 2003, the Three Gorges Dam was constructed, displacing over one million residents. Included in those impacted was my grandmother’s entire family. My grandmother grew up in Old Fengdu City, now hosting fishermen during low-tide seasons and entirely submerged the rest of the year. For 5 weeks, I traveled across this region, attempting to 发现三峡 (“Discover the Three Gorges” in a far less poetic translation). This period of time turned into a meditation on language. I communicated in broken Chinese, composed photographs with elementary grammar, and rekindled familial ties previously unknown to me across our differing nationalities.
